Paris, France

My trip to Paris exceeded all expectations! The Eiffel Tower at sunset was absolutely breathtaking, and the local cafes in Montmartre were a delightful experience. I stayed in the Latin Quarter and found it very convenient to reach all the major attractions. Make sure to visit the Louvre early in the morning to avoid crowds.

Nurbek Assylbek
April 2, 2025
Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto is a magical blend of tradition and modernity. The Fushimi Inari Shrine was absolutely stunning with its thousands of vermilion torii gates. Walking through the bamboo forest in Arashiyama felt like stepping into another world. I recommend getting a rental bicycle to explore the city as many attractions are spread out. The only downside was some areas being extremely crowded during cherry blossom season.

Ibragim Saiffulin
March 24, 2025
Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is a perfect mix of beach, architecture, and amazing food! Gaudi's masterpieces are even more impressive in person - the Sagrada Familia left me speechless. The Gothic Quarter's narrow streets are perfect for wandering and discovering hidden gems. I spent evenings at the waterfront enjoying sangria and watching street performers. Tip: Buy tickets for popular attractions online in advance to skip lines.

Alnur Shldenov
April 10, 2025
Grand Canyon, USA

The Grand Canyon is truly majestic, and the views are incredible. I hiked part of the South Kaibab Trail which offered stunning vistas. However, I'm giving this 3 stars because the tourist facilities were overcrowded and somewhat overpriced. Also, parking was a nightmare - arrive very early or use the shuttle service. If you're planning to hike, make sure to bring plenty of water and sun protection as there's very little shade.
